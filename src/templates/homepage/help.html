WE ARE READY TO HELP

Welcome to the comprehensive Help and Support guide for the Library Management System. We understand that navigating a library's vast offerings and digital tools can sometimes be a challenge. This detailed guide aims to provide you with step-by-step instructions and explanations for various aspects of our system. If you require further assistance, our dedicated support team is just a click away.

Getting Started

How to Log In

1. Navigate to the Library Management System website.

2. Click on the "Log In" button at the top-right corner of the page.

3. Enter your username and password.

4. Click the "Log In" button.

Dashboard Overview

Searching Books

1. Navigate to the "Searching" section to explore our extensive collection.

2. Use the search bar to find books by title, author, category, or sourcetype..

3. Refine your search results using filters such as publication year or availability.

Book Details - Status

1. Click on a book's title, or book cover image to access detailed information

2. In the book detail page, all of the information of book such as title, author, summary,... will be shown

3. Check the "Availability" section to see the number of copies currently on the shelf and the borrowing period.

Your Account Settings

Updating Personal Information

1. Click on your profile and choose edit profile

2. Update your personal details, including your contact information.

3. Click "Save Changes" to update your account.

Changing Password

1. Enter the current password.

2. Set the new password for account

3. Confirm the password and "Save change"

Changing Profile Picture

1. Click to change picture.

2. Choose a picture from your browser and "Save change".
